# Reflection

## High-Energy Pop vs Chill Lofi
---High-Energy Pop pushes upbeat tracks like Sunrise City and Gym Hero to the top. Chill Lofi shifts to calmer songs like Library Rain and Midnight Coding. This makes sense because both mood and target energy are very different between the two profiles.

## High-Energy Pop vs Deep Intense Rock
---Both profiles still keep some high-energy songs near the top. The main difference is that Deep Intense Rock promotes Storm Runner first, while High-Energy Pop starts with Sunrise City. Gym Hero appears in both because its energy is very close to both targets.

## Chill Lofi vs Deep Intense Rock
---Chill Lofi rewards low-energy chill songs, while Deep Intense Rock rewards intense high-energy songs. Their top songs are mostly different, which is expected. The overlap is small and usually happens when energy closeness is strong enough to keep a song in the top 5.

## Why Gym Hero Keeps Showing Up
---Gym Hero has very high energy, and energy has a strong weight in the current scoring setup. So even if the profile is not exactly "Happy Pop," Gym Hero can still score well from energy closeness alone. In simple terms, the system is currently very sensitive to energy and that can overpower vibe in some cases.
